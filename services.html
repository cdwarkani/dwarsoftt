How we do?



  • Initiation
  • Planning
  • Execution
  • Management

At Dwarsoft we believe that initiation is the first phase of the project lifecycle. This is where the project’s value and feasibility are measured by our team. At this phase we make sure that every team member is completely engaged with the project, all of the stakeholders fully understand the details and requirements and all of the main goals to be achieved are set.

At this phase we make a solid plan to guide the team, as well as keep them on time and on budget.We believe that a well-written project plan gives guidance for obtaining resources, acquiring financing and procuring required materials. The project plan gives our team a direction for producing quality outputs, handling risk, creating acceptance, communicating benefits to stakeholders and managing suppliers.

Execution is all about building deliverables that satisfy the customer.At this phase our team leaders make this happen by allocating resources and keeping team members focused on their assigned tasks.Execution relies heavily on the planning phase. The work and efforts of the team during the execution phase are derived from the project plan.

We believe that as teams execute their project plan, they must constantly manage their progress.To guarantee delivery of what was promised to the client, we manage and analyze all the tasks everyday to prevent scope creep, calculate key performance indicators and track variations from allotted cost and time.We believe that this constant vigilance helps keep the project moving ahead smoothly.
